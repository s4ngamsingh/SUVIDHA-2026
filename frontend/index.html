SUVIDHA 2026

About the Project

Developed as part of the SUVIDHA 2026 national hackathon organized by C-DAC under MeitY, Government of India, this project aims to bring citizen-centric digital governance to urban utility offices through intuitive kiosk-based solutions.

Creator Information

This solution is designed and developed as a smart city innovation project, focusing on accessibility, scalability, and real-world governance challenges faced by urban citizens.

Built using modern web technologies with a strong emphasis on UI/UX, performance, and security.
